STAY SAFE ONLINE – PREVENTION CHECKLIST

To stay safe from online scams and cyber fraud, especially while using digital payments, it is important to follow some basic safety steps:

• Never share your OTP or PIN with anyone, no matter who they claim to be.
• Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.
• Always check and verify payment requests before approving them.
• Try to use only official and trusted apps for transactions.
• Enable two-factor authentication for extra security.
• Regularly check your bank statements to notice any unusual activity.
• Avoid using public Wi-Fi when making payments or transactions.
• Keep your apps updated so that they have the latest security features.

Financial Safety Tips:

• Never approve “collect requests” without checking who sent them.
• Do not trust unknown callers asking for money or payment details.
