### DIGITAL SURVIVAL KIT FOR FRESHERS



###### 1\. What is Digital Literacy?



Digital literacy means knowing how to use technology and the internet in a smart and responsible way.

It helps students use online tools for studying, communicating, and building their future careers.



In simple words, it is about using technology safely, correctly, and effectively.



###### 2\. Essential Digital Tools



These are some important tools that every fresher should know about:



• Google Docs – Useful for writing assignments and working together with classmates.

• GitHub – A platform where students can upload coding projects and build a portfolio.

• LinkedIn – Helps in professional networking and connecting with people in different fields.

• Google Drive – A safe place to store important files, notes, and documents online.



These tools make studying and collaboration much easier.



###### 3\. Safe Internet Practices



While using the internet, students should always be careful. Some important safety tips are:



• Use strong and unique passwords for accounts.

• Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.

• Turn on two-factor authentication for extra security.

• Avoid using public Wi-Fi for important or personal work.



Following these steps can help protect your personal information online.



###### 4\. Professional Online Presence



Today, your online profile is like your digital identity. So it is important to keep it professional.



• Use your real name and a clear profile photo.

• Keep your profiles clean and respectful.

• Share achievements, skills, and learning experiences instead of personal drama.



A good online presence can help in internships and future opportunities.



###### 5\. Email Etiquette



Writing proper emails is an important skill for students.



• Always write a clear and proper subject line.

• Be polite and respectful in your message.

• Avoid using slang or short forms.

• End the email with a proper signature (Name, course, etc.).



Good email communication creates a positive impression.
