# Digital Survival Kit for Freshers



### Project Overview



The project “Digital Survival Kit for Freshers” is designed to help first-year students understand and develop essential digital skills required in today’s world. As students enter college, they are exposed to various digital platforms, tools, and communication methods. This project acts as a beginner-friendly guide to help them use these resources effectively.



The project focuses on key areas such as digital literacy, professional communication, use of online platforms, and cyber safety. It provides practical knowledge through real tasks like creating profiles, solving coding problems, writing emails, and understanding cybercrime.



The main objective of this project is to help students become confident, responsible, and smart digital users.



\--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



### Project Objectives



1. To understand the concept of digital literacy  
2. To learn about important digital tools used by students  
3. To build a professional digital presence  
4. To improve communication skills through formal emails  
5. To create awareness about cybercrime and online safety

### Module Summaries



##### Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic



In this task, I created an infographic titled “Digital Survival Kit for Freshers” using PowerPoint presentation. The infographic presents important information such as the meaning of digital literacy, commonly used digital tools like GitHub, Kaggle, and Stack Overflow, safe internet practices, and email etiquette.



The goal was to make the content simple, visually appealing, and easy to understand for freshers. This task helped me learn how to present information in a clear and creative way using visual design.



📁 Folder: `task-1-presentation`



\--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



##### Task 2: Digital Portfolio



In this task, I created my digital portfolio by setting up profiles on GitHub, Kaggle, and Stack Overflow. These platforms are important for students to build their technical identity and improve their skills.



GitHub is used to upload and manage projects, Kaggle is used for practicing data-related skills, and Stack Overflow helps in solving programming doubts. This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional online presence.



I plan to continue using these platforms throughout my academic journey to improve my skills and showcase my work.



📁 Folder: `task-2-portfolio`



\--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



##### Task 3: Platforms \& Collaboration



In this task, I explored coding and collaboration platforms. I created an account on HackerRank and completed a beginner-level problem called “Solve Me First,” which helped me understand basic coding concepts.



I also created a Google Form titled “Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz” with five questions. This helped me learn how to create surveys and collect responses using Google Sheets.



This task improved my understanding of practical digital tools that can be used for learning and collaboration.



📁 Folder: `task-3-platforms`



\--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



##### Task 4: Email Etiquette \& Online Behavior



In this task, I learned about professional communication by writing two formal emails—one requesting an assignment extension and another applying for an internship opportunity.



I also created a Social Media Do’s and Don’ts checklist to promote responsible online behavior. This task helped me understand the importance of clear communication, proper email structure, and maintaining professionalism online.



📁 Folder: `task-4-email-etiquette`



\--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------



##### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness



In this task, I studied a real case of UPI fraud and understood how cybercrimes happen. I also created a prevention checklist with safety tips for students, including financial safety measures.



This task made me more aware of online threats and taught me how to protect my personal and financial information while using digital platforms.



📁 Folder: `task-5-cybercrime`
