DIGITAL LITERACY AWARENESS QUIZ

Title: Are You Digitally Smart? – Fresher Quiz

Description:
This quiz is designed to test basic knowledge about digital literacy, online safety, and professional digital skills. It is helpful for first-year students to understand how to use digital platforms responsibly.

--------------------------------------------------

Question 1:
What is digital literacy?

A) Using social media all day  
B) Using technology safely and effectively  
C) Playing online games  
D) Fixing computers  

Correct Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

Question 2:
Which platform is mainly used to share coding projects?

A) Instagram  
B) WhatsApp  
C) GitHub  
D) Facebook  

Correct Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

Question 3:
What should you do before clicking on an unknown link?

A) Click it quickly  
B) Ignore everything  
C) Verify the source  
D) Share it with others  

Correct Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

Question 4:
Which of the following shows good email etiquette?

A) Using slang words  
B) No subject line  
C) Clear subject and polite language  
D) Sending incomplete message  

Correct Answer: C

--------------------------------------------------

Question 5:
What is a common sign of a UPI scam?

A) Receiving money  
B) Being asked to share OTP or approve unknown requests  
C) Using bank apps  
D) Checking balance  

Correct Answer: B

--------------------------------------------------

End of Quiz
