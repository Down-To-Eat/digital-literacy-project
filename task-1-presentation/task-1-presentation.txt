DIGITAL SURVIVAL KIT FOR FRESHERS

--------------------------------------------------

WHAT IS DIGITAL LITERACY?
Using technology in a smart, safe, and effective way for learning, communication, and growth.

--------------------------------------------------

TOP DIGITAL TOOLS FOR STUDENTS

• Google Docs – Write and collaborate on assignments  
• GitHub – Store and showcase coding projects  
• Kaggle – Practice data science and work with datasets  
• Stack Overflow – Ask questions and learn from developers  
• Google Drive – Store files safely online  

--------------------------------------------------

STAY SAFE ONLINE

• Use strong passwords  
• Never share OTP or personal details  
• Avoid suspicious links  
• Enable two-factor authentication  

--------------------------------------------------

BUILD A PROFESSIONAL PROFILE

• Use real name and proper photo  
• Keep your profile clean and formal  
• Share your skills and achievements  

--------------------------------------------------

EMAIL ETIQUETTE

• Write clear subject lines  
• Be polite and respectful  
• Avoid slang or shortcuts  
• End with your name and details
