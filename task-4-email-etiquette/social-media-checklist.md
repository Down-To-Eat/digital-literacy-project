### SOCIAL MEDIA DO’s AND DON’Ts FOR STUDENTS



Social media is something most students use every day. But it’s important to use it wisely because what we post online can affect our image and even future opportunities.



\--------------------------------------------------



### DO’s:



• Keep your profile professional  

Make sure your profile picture, username, and bio look clean and appropriate. This creates a good impression on others.



• Share useful and positive content  

Post things related to your studies, achievements, skills, or useful information instead of unnecessary or negative content.



• Respect others online  

Always be polite while commenting or messaging. Treat others with respect just like you would in real life.



• Use privacy settings  

Adjust your account settings to control who can see your posts and personal information. This helps protect your privacy.



• Think before you post  

Before uploading anything, ask yourself if it is appropriate and safe. Remember that what you post online stays for a long time.



\--------------------------------------------------



### DON’Ts:



• Do not share personal information publicly  

Avoid sharing sensitive details like phone number, address, passwords, or bank details on social media.



• Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  

Do not post anything that can hurt others or damage your reputation.



• Do not engage in online arguments  

Avoid unnecessary fights or negative discussions, as they can harm your image and mental peace.



• Avoid spreading fake news  

Always verify information before sharing. Spreading false information can create confusion and problems.



• Do not misuse others’ content  

Do not copy or share someone else’s work without permission. Always give proper credit.



\--------------------------------------------------



### Conclusion:



Using social media in a responsible way is very important for students. A good online presence can help in studies and career opportunities, while careless use can create problems. Therefore, students should always be careful, respectful, and responsible while using social media.
