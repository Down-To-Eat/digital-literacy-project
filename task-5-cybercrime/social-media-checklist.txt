SOCIAL MEDIA DO’s AND DON’Ts

Social media is something most students use every day. But it’s important to use it wisely because what we post online can affect our image and even future opportunities.

DO’s :

• Keep your profile neat and a bit professional.
• Share useful, positive, or meaningful content.
• Be respectful when interacting with others online.
• Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts.
• Always think before posting anything.

DON’Ts :

• Do not share personal information like phone numbers, addresses, or passwords publicly.
• Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
• Try not to get involved in unnecessary online arguments.
• Do not share fake news without checking if it is true.
• Do not copy or misuse someone else’s content without permission.
